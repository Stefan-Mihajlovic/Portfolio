ABOUT ME

Well... How do I start this?

Hey there! I'm Stefan Mihajlovic, a programming enthusiast from Belgrade, Serbia. I've been coding since I was 14, and I've developed a serious passion for creating beautiful responsive websites and immersive video games.

I love programming because it allows me to dive into complex problems and come up with creative solutions. It's like a puzzle that never gets old, and I'm always excited to see what I can come up with next.

One of my favorite things about programming is collaboration. I firmly believe that great things can be achieved when people work together, and I'm always excited to collaborate with others on a project. Whether it's brainstorming ideas, writing code, or playtesting a game, I'm happy to contribute and work towards a shared goal.
