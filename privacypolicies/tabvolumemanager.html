Tab Volume Manager Privacy Policy

Tab Volume Manager is a browser extension that allows users to control the volume and apply audio effects (such as bass boost and voice clarity) on a per-tab basis. We take user privacy seriously and aim to be fully transparent about the data we collect, store, and use.

1. Information We Collect

Tab Volume Manager does not collect or transmit any personal data or browsing history to any external servers or third-party services. All data used by the extension is stored locally on the user's device using Chrome’s built-in storage.local API.

2. How We Use Information

The information stored is solely used to: Restore user preferences for each website, Apply volume and audio effects across sessions, Maintain a consistent user experience (e.g., dark mode). We do not track, log, or monitor user activity in any way.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. All data remains strictly local to your browser.
